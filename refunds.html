Returns and Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2023

Thank you for shopping at KpopKraze. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you are not entirely happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free returns and refund process. Please read the following policy carefully to understand how returns and refunds are processed.

Returns Eligibility

1. Eligible Items:

We accept returns for items that meet the following criteria:

  • The item is in its original condition, unopened, and unused.
  • The return request is initiated within [number of days, e.g., 14 days] from the date of delivery.
  • The item is not listed under the "Non-Returnable" category (e.g., personalized items, digital downloads, etc.).

2. Non-Eligible Items:

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Products marked as "Non-Returnable" on the product page.
  • Items that have been used, damaged, or altered after delivery.

Return Process

1. Return Shipping:

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except in cases where KpopKraze has shipped the wrong or damaged item. We recommend using a trackable shipping method for returns and keeping the shipping receipt as proof of return.

Refund Process

1. Refund Eligibility:

Upon receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will verify its eligibility for a refund based on the Returns Eligibility criteria mentioned above.

2. Refund Methods:

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow [number of days, e.g., 5-10 business days] for the refund to be processed and reflect in your account.

Exchanges

KpopKraze does not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the standard return process to return the original item and place a new order for the desired item.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will arrange for a return or replacement at no additional cost to you.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Returns and Refund Policy or need assistance with the return process, please contact our customer support team at info@kpopkraze.com

By placing an order with KpopKraze, you agree to the terms of this Returns and Refund Policy.
